All your data either meta data or binary files will store on kii storage, so that you have to make relation your cubeacon account with kii account.

The integration steps are as follows :

  1. Open access keys on your kii developer site
  2. Fill your access key (from kii apps).
  3. Generate token.
  4. Save setting. Note : Client ID and Client Secret are not saved in our storage.
