"With a low price you got more powerful feature and secure data access."

Cubeacon provides easy to configure more than hundred beacons, easy to tracking point with indoor map and geo fencing for tracking developer group areas like retail shop group area. User could manage smart advertising by simply uploading attractive image brochure. For secure cloud storage data, cubeacon using a third party backend as a service called KiiCloud
