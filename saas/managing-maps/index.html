We know that micro location or usually called indoor mapping is very useful. Imagine that we are a customer who visit supermarket. There are a lot of space, and we will to find area that we want. What happen if there is no maps? It needs a big effort to look for them. Cubeacon answer that issue. Just go to maps menu then customize beacon point, users will know where they are.

For more details, follow the steps below :

  1. choose maps menu.
  2. click add button.
  3. fill your map data.
  4. you can edit map if there is any wrong informations.
  5. bind beacons to map with click bind button.
  6. drag beacon list in the right sidebar.
  7. save binding.
