The goal of advertisement is order to make known our products by others. High quality advertising heavily dependent on content and methods. Users do not need to think about them. Cubeacon is the solution for that case. User could manage smart advertising by simply uploading attractive image brochure. So it would be a colorful brochure.

For more details, follow the steps below :

  1. Choose brochures menu.
  2. Click add button.
  3. Fill your brochure data.
  4. You can edit brochure if there is any wrong informations.
  5. Bind beacons to brochure with click bind button.
  6. Save binding.
