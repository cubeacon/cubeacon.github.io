In this section we will explain detail about how to managing apps. It is the first step before creating beacons. With apps we can build a pathways for communication between SDK and Data at cubeacon services. You will get Client ID and Client secret after creation process.

For more details, follow the steps below :

  1. Choose apps menu.
  2. Fill your basic information.
  3. Fill your advance information.
  4. Click button save.
  5. Download binary SDK.
