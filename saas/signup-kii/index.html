Secure storage services is an ideal of cloud storage services. Cubeacon with the low price service packages use third party called KiiCloud to answer that issue. So that user must have Kii Developer Account before access cubeacon services.

It is very easy to sign up, the steps are as follows :

  1. go to KiiCloud Developer site.
  2. click sign up button.
  3. fill yours details.
  4. log in with your account.
  5. create apps.
  6. get access key.
