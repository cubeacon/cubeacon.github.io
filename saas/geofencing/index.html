Geofencing makes easy to find your area, be friendly with customer, say greeting with customer in a radius around a store or point location.

To enjoy this feature, please follow steps below :

  1. Click geofencing menu
  2. Add new group
  3. Bind group
  4. Seach your location area
  5. Choose your area in the map by using one click.
  6. Click save to store latitude and longitude.
